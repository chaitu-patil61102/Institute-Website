Course Overview

Security auditing is the process of evaluating systems, policies, and operations to ensure compliance with industry standards. This course teaches risk assessment, control testing, and audit report writing.

You'll explore ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST, and PCI-DSS frameworks and gain skills in identifying security gaps and recommending remediations. Simulated audit scenarios help apply your learning.

Ideal for auditors, compliance officers, IT managers, and cybersecurity professionals.
